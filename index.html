Engineering AI for Extreme Environments

I am a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence Applications at the Graduate School of Software Convergence, Kookmin University, and a full-time resident researcher at the Special Communication Convergence Service Research Center (SCRC).

My research focuses on Marine DX-based Physical AI, with an emphasis on developing intelligent systems that operate reliably in extreme and resource-constrained environments such as underwater and polar communication networks.

"Bridging advanced AI models with practical, system-level implementation at the edge"

In parallel, I have a strong interest in the architectural design and optimization of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Small Language Models (SLMs), particularly for edge deployment.
