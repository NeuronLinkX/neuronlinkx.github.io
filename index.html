About My Research

AI Researcher | Kookmin University

I am a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence Applications at the Graduate School of Software Convergence, Kookmin University. My research is focused on advancing edge AI technologies and optimizing lightweight language models (SLM/LLM) for resource-constrained communication environments.

My work bridges the gap between cutting-edge AI development and practical implementation in challenging real-world scenarios, particularly in special communication networks such as underwater and polar environments. I am passionate about creating efficient, scalable AI solutions that can operate effectively where computational resources are limited.
